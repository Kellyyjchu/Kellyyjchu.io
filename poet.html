Who is Nikita Gill?

Nikita Gill is an author and poet who initially found fame publishing her poems on Instagram to her over 750,000 followers. Gill, who has published eight volumes of poetry, several short story collections, and a novel, frequently examines love, identity, sexuality, and mental health with her work.
